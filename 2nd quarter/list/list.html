Ordered List (ol) - Steps to Bake a Cake

  2. Gather ingredients
  3. Mix dry ingredients
  4. Mix wet ingredients
  5. Combine wet and dry
  6. Pour into baking pan
  7. Bake in the oven
  8. Let it cool
  9. Frost the cake
  10. Serve and enjoy!
